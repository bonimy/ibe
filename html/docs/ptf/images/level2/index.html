Overview

Products derived from combining the Level 1 data, such as deep sky coadded data and the catalogs derived from such data. The coadded mosaic is sometimes referred to as the "Level-2 Coadd", or "coadded image", or even "reference image". No Level 2 data products are currently publicly available; all Level 2 data are proprietary. If you have proprietary access to these data, you must log in to be able to see or download these data.
